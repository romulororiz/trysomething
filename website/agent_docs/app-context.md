# TrySomething — Full App Context

## What is TrySomething?

TrySomething is a hobby discovery platform that uses AI to help people find, start, and stick with new hobbies. It solves the "I want to try something new but don't know what or how to start" problem.

## Core user journey

1. **Discovery** — User answers a few questions about their interests, schedule, budget, personality
2. **Matching** — AI analyzes responses and suggests hobbies ranked by compatibility
3. **Matched Hobbies Screen** — User sees their top hobby matches with compatibility scores and reasons why each was matched
4. **Roadmap** — Each hobby has a step-by-step roadmap: what to buy, where to start, first project, milestones
5. **AI Coach** — Personalized tips and encouragement as the user progresses through their hobby roadmap
6. **Progress Tracking** — Check off milestones, see your journey, celebrate wins

## What makes it unique

- It's NOT a list of hobbies. It's a personalized matching engine + guided onboarding for each hobby.
- The AI coach adapts to your pace, budget, location, and learning style.
- The roadmap breaks down intimidating hobbies into approachable steps. "Learn guitar" becomes "Day 1: Tune your strings. Day 3: Learn 2 chords. Week 2: Play your first song."
- It treats starting a hobby like onboarding into a product — smooth, guided, confidence-building.

## Target audience

- Adults (25-45) who feel stuck in routines
- People who say "I wish I had a hobby" but never start
- Career-focused people looking for work-life balance
- Someone scrolling their phone thinking "there must be more than this"

## Key value propositions (for the landing page)

1. **AI-powered matching** — Not random suggestions. Personalized to YOU.
2. **Step-by-step roadmaps** — Never feel lost. Know exactly what to do next.
3. **AI coaching** — Like having a patient friend who's an expert in everything.
4. **Zero overwhelm** — Start in 5 minutes, not 5 hours of research.
5. **Real progress** — Track milestones, build momentum, see yourself grow.
